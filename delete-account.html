Delete Account - RefZone

Last updated: 14 April 2026

This page explains how RefZone users can request deletion of their account and related personal data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an e-mail to refzoneaz@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete RefZone Account.
  3. Include the e-mail address used in your RefZone account and, if available, your full name and license number.

What data will be deleted

  • Account profile information such as name, e-mail address, role, and license number.
  • Authentication-related account access associated with the user profile.
  • Personal in-app data that is directly linked to the account, where deletion is technically and legally possible.

What data may be retained

  • Records that must be retained for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or administrative reasons.
  • Operational match history or audit records when retention is required to preserve league or competition integrity.
  • Backup copies that are automatically removed after the normal backup retention period.

Retention period

We aim to review deletion requests within 30 days. Some limited records may be retained longer if required by law, security obligations, dispute resolution, or legitimate operational needs.

Contact

If you have questions about account deletion, contact: refzoneaz@gmail.com
