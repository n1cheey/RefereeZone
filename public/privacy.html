Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 April 2026

RefZone ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RefZone mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: name, email address, and other information you provide during registration.
  • Professional Data: referee role, assignments, match history, and related basketball officiating data.
  • Device Information: device type, operating system, and basic technical data.
  • Usage Data: interactions with the app, features used, and performance data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the RefZone platform
  • Manage referee and table official assignments
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Communicate important updates related to the service

3. Data Storage and Processing

Your data is stored and processed using Supabase, a backend service that provides database and authentication infrastructure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the app, or when required by law.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
