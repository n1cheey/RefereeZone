Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 April 2026

RefZone ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RefZone mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: name, email address, and other information you provide during registration.
  • Professional Data: referee role, assignments, match history, and related basketball officiating data.
  • Device Information: device type, operating system, and basic technical data.
  • Usage Data: interactions with the app, features used, and performance data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the RefZone platform
  • Manage referee and table official assignments
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Communicate important updates related to the service

3. Data Storage and Processing

Your data is stored and processed using Supabase, a backend service that provides database and authentication infrastructure.

Supabase may process data on secure servers and follows industry-standard security practices.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With authorized platform administrators (e.g., league officials)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of users or the platform

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Children's Privacy

RefZone is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Supabase for backend functionality. These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
